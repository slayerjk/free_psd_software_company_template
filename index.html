Organize Your Files

Being creative with your work is one thing, but with complex projects requiring multiple elements to get and running, it's nice to see that Adobe have spent some time tweaking and improving the management and organization tools of Photoshop.


Organize Images

If your intranet resources include several folders of digital pictures-thousands of shots taken for catalogs, perhaps-and you've ever wasted time opening one picture and then another trying to find the one you need.


The File browser lets you scan through your images visually, by creating thumbnails of a whole folder. To open it, select File/Browser from the pulldown menu. The File Browser is initially in the palette well in the upper right corner, so you'll want to drag in into the center of the desktop. Resize it to offer more coverage. You'll see your folders displaying in a tree hierarachy in the upper left corner, with the contents of each folder in the larger page to the right.
